Gaussian Processes

Gaussian Processes (GPs) are powerful non-parametric models used for regression and probabilistic inference. They provide uncertainty estimates alongside predictions, which makes them especially suited for scientific applications.

I'm applying Gaussian Process techniques in Cosmology, particularly to model data from Gamma Ray Bursts (GRBs) as a window into the early universe.
